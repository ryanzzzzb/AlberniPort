Why There?

Tsunamis can be a serious threat to the Port Alberni harbor. Transform
boundaries near the Juan de Fuca and North American plate can trigger tsunamis.
This might not sound as important because Port Alberni is in the middle of
vancouver island but it has happened before. In addition, all the highways
leading to port alberni are surrounded or built in mountains. Therefore, if
natural disasters such as floods/earthquakes take place, it might block the
exits leading to more severe damage. On the other hand, this place is also prone
to wildfires because of the dryness and human activities. The geographic position
of Port Alberni makes wildfires easier to spread because of strong winds, steep hills,
and densely packed trees.
