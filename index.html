Where is Port Alberni?

Port Alberni is a city located on the western coast of British Columbia in Vancouver Island.
Its absolute location is 49.2° North and 124.0° West. The city is in the Alberni Valley which
is in the north of Alberni Inlet. The city has two official city centers, being divided by
the Rogers Creek. There are also many creeks and ravines that intersect the city. The population
of Port Alberni is 18,259. Some physical features and activities include the Alberni port quay,
Alberni valley museum, Alberni Pacific railway, Port Alberni maritime discovery center, and the
“Hole in the wall".
